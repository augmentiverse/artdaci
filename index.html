Augmented Atlas Model

A printed book first. A spatial museum when the reader wants more.

DACIART is designed as a rich paper atlas of masterpieces: each printed spread can stand alone with historical context, close-looking questions, technique notes, timelines, symbols, restoration stories, and comparison prompts. The digital layer extends the book with narrated AR, 3D models, video, QR access, and spatial placement.

Read Museum-style printed pages with enough context to enjoy the work without a screen.
Scan Optional AR reveals audio, labels, videos, and 3D interpretations over the painting.
Place Spatial mode lets readers position models in their own room and build a personal gallery.
Teach QR links, accessibility options, and classroom prompts make the book usable at home or in school.
